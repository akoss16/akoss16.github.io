Views and Vistas

The top of Bernal Heights Summit hosts a panoramic view of the City, with the buildings below miniaturized and reminding me of a model set that I could reach out and pick up. You can see the Golden Gate, Bay and San Mateo Bridges, Mount Diablo to the East and Twin Peaks to the West.
