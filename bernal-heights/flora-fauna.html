Flora and Fauna

Having one of the most pleasant climates and many micro-climates, Bernal Heights can grow most anything. Many residents grow food, have fruit trees, and there is an abundance of flowers and beautiful plantings to be seen. Gardeners do not have a break year-round!

Aside from dogs of all kinds, and cats (with their human caregivers, of course), Bernal is home to racoons, skunks, possums, squirrels, many kinds of birds, and a stop-over for visiting coyotes, a great blue heron, and the Telegraph Hill parrots.
